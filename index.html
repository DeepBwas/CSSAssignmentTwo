Fairytale

Alexander Rybak

"Fairytale" is a song by the Belarusian-Norwegian singer and violinist Alexander Rybak with which he won the Eurovision Song Contest in 2009 representing Norway. It received the highest total score in Eurovision history (387 out of 492). The song was released on January 12th, 2009, as the first single from his debut studio album, Fairytales.
